about me

I’ve always been very athletic. You name it, I’ve played it; volleyball, basketball, and a lot of soccer. Unfortunately, like many other athletes, I never fully appreciated the value of proper training practices and stretching, which led to injury and the end of my athletic career. While I felt very strong and powerful, my overall flexibility was extremely limited, and I only ever dreamed of touching my toes. This experience led me to educate myself on the intricacies of the human body and how to have a more well-rounded training practice. I now aspire to educate others in the same manner, and to help them develop a full and healthy understanding of their fitness practices.

services

Improve your play and reduce your risk of injury through:


Fascial Stretch Therapy

Table based, assisted stretching focusing on the fascial lines, that will increase overall mobility and flexibility. Using Stretch to Win ™ techniques, the goal is to increase ranges of motion to create a greater amount of mobility while also retaining stability throughout the whole body. This service is excellent in preparation for competition, injury prevention and rehabilitation, people new to training and those who have plateaued in regards to their fitness goals.

Available in 60 and 90 minute sessions


Mobility sessions

Guided fascial release therapy where a variety of tools from foam rollers, bands, dowels and balls are used. Based on the individual’s current fitness goals, can be adjusted to be used before or after training sessions.

Available in 30 and 60 minute sessions

Personal training sessions

One-on-one training sessions in which an individualized program is designed to achieve your fitness and health goals.

Available in 60 minute sessions


Partner training

Ideal for couples or friends with similar fitness goals. Programs are designed with both participants’ strengths and weaknesses in mind. For those that believe that “misery loves company”.

Available in 60 minute sessions


Group training
(4 participants max)

For groups with similar fitness goals ranging from sports teams to families planning their active-oriented travels together.

Available in 60 minute sessions
